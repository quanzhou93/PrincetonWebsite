My Senior Thesis: H.E.L.P.: Highly (E)nquisive Learning Probe

Itâ€™s an Autonomous robot that asks people for help when it gets lost!

My Senior thesis concentrates on creating a robot that autonomously navigates unknown indoor environments with SLAM techinques. When lost, it asks humans to help lead it close to the goal location. This project involves collision avoidance, skeletal detection, and particle filtering. Metadata from the Kinect is digested by SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) techniques that drive decision making processes. The whole system is nicely glued together with ROS (Robotics Operating System).

Thanks to the awesome researchers who have worked on this system (Turtlebot group), I have an awesome project base from which to launch my thesis.
