Welcome to my website! I am currently a Senior in the Mechanical and Aerospace Department at Princeton, and I love to build things. I spend a lot of my time and energy working teaching Hack Classes, which tries to encourage cool side-project. So far, we've held a 8-hour workshop , a Wintercession class on Data Science, and several series of classes in Web Development. I'm most excited about classes in the spring semester, where we'll be tying a lot of other related technologies such as web automation, scraping, and data science into the mix.
