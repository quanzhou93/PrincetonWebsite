From the Author:

Welcome to my website! I am currently a Senior in the Mechanical and Aerospace Department at Princeton, and I love to build things. Like many other Senior undergraduates in MAE, I spend most of his time foiling my schedule's attempts to kill me. In the coveted lulls between my problem sets, coding assignments, and his responsibilities as the Vice-President of the Princeton Charter Club, I enjoy running 2-3 miles everyday, watching the Big Bang Theory, and crashing with his friends in some good 'ol fashion video gaming.

I created this website as part of a project of how to learn website development. It took me 1-2 hours everyday for about a month to learn the HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap Framework to erect these pages, and I hope that you enjoy this modest little website. If you want to check out a cooler looking one, visit www.princetonwebdev.org for an elegant website that I created as the founder of the 'Princeton Website Development Workshop.' It took me about 6-8 hours to get to get this static webpage up and running w/ google forms as control for our sign-ups. Our event had over 80 people sign up, and 20 georgeous websites featured at our website gallery. I look foward to organizing it again in the spring due to the overwhelming wave of interest as a result of this workshop.
